        Who should use Ninja Menu?
    </b></h3>
    <p>
    Anyone who wants to improve sales by making the menu more attractive and easier-to-use. No coding or technical skill required.</p>
</div>
<div>
    <h3><b>
        Why should I use Ninja Menus?
    </b></h3>
    <div class="panel" style="display: block;">
        <p>What is more important to the sale than being able to manage your menu in your own style, making it special, unique and eye-catching but still user-friendly? Our Magento 2 Ninja mega menu is absolutely what you are looking for to improve your menu layout.</p>
    </div>
</div>
<div>
    <h3><b>Will Ninja Menus affect my website's speed?</b></h3>
    <p>
    Of course not. Being cache supported means no need to make an HTTP request to load assets like scripts, CSS, and images anymore. Also, your websites can serve content in next to no time, improve both front-end and back-end load times, and reduce stress on the website’s origin server.</p>
</div>
<div>
    <h3><b>
        How to add the background to the submenu?
    </b></h3>
    <p>With the help of Ninja Menus, adding image background for the submenu is now a piece of cake. Just by following some easy steps. Take a look at our User guide to get <a href="https://magezon.com/pub/media/productfile/ninjamenus-v2.0.0-user_guides.pdf">more details</a>.</p>
</div>
<div>
    <h3><b>
        How to Configure menu item for Multiple Store view? 
    </b></h3>
    <p>Just by choosing any language in “All store view" drop-down list. More details <a href="https://magezon.com/pub/media/productfile/ninjamenus-v2.0.0-user_guides.pdf">here</a>.</p>
</div>
<div>
    <h3><b>
        How to import Store Categories?
    </b></h3>
    <p>In Menu Builder mode, click on Import store categories button -> You will see a pop-up with our Default categories inside. Choose the categories you would like to add on your menu and then click Import. Make sure you understand our Import Children button. To have a closer look, click <a href="https://magezon.com/pub/media/productfile/ninjamenus-v2.0.0-user_guides.pdf">here</a>.</p>
</div>
<div>
    <h3><b>
        What does “100% responsive” mean? 
    </b></h3>
    <p>
    It means your customers are now able to visit and shop around your site more easily. No matter what kind of device they are using, your store can be displayed perfectly. No more zoom in and out to have look at your products on mobiles. As a result, they will eager to visit your site that makes your sales boosted.</p>
</div>
<div>
    <h3><b>
        How many languages can Ninja Menus support?
    </b></h3>
    <p>We supported all the languages listed in the drop-down list of All store view.</p>
</div>
<div>
    <h3><b>
        Is it possible to have the menu sticky with this extension? 
    </b></h3>
    <p>We've already added this function in our extension. Make your menu stick to the top by switching only 1 button in the backend. (We described in the <a href="https://magezon.com/pub/media/productfile/ninjamenus-v2.0.0-user_guides.pdf">user guide</a>).</p>
</div>
